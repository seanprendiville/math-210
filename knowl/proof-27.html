Proof
We proceed by induction on \(n\text{.}\) The base case \(k_1 \geq 1\) is true because \(k_1 \in \N\text{.}\) As induction hypothesis, suppose that \(k_{n-1} \geq n-1\text{.}\) Since \(k_n \gt k_{n-1}\) we have that \(k_n \geq k_{n-1} +1 \geq (n-1) + 1 = n\text{.}\) This proves the induction step, completing the proof.
