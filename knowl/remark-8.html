Remark 3.22. Proving a sequence does not have a limit using subsequences.
Proposition 3.20 is useful if one wants to show that a sequence \((a_n)_n\) does not have a limit. Using Proposition 3.20, it suffices to find two subsequences \((a_{k_n})_n\) and \((a_{l_n})_n\) which have different limits.
