Let us first prove the "only if" or "\(\impliedby\)" direction. Fix \(\eps \gt 0\text{.}\) By definition of convergence (DefinitionÂ 1.23) there exists \(N = N(\eps/2) \in \N\) such that for all \(n \geq N\) we have \(|a_n - a| \leq \eps/2\text{.}\) Thus by the triangle inequality, if \(n, m\geq N\) then

\begin{equation*} |a_n -a_m| \leq |a_n-a| + |a-a_m| \leq \eps/2 + \eps/2 = \eps. \end{equation*}

Hence the sequence is Cauchy.
