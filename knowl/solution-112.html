Solution 7.22.d.1.
Using the previous part, we obtain a sequence \((x_n)_{n=1}^\infty\) such that \(x_n \to 0\) (by the sandwich rule) and \(f(x_n)\leq 0\) for all \(n\in \N\text{.}\) By continuity and the inequality rule for sequential limits, we thereby deduce that
\begin{equation*} f(0) = \lim_{n\to \infty} f(x_n) \leq 0. \end{equation*}
