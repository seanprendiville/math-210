By the multiplication rule for absolutely convergent series (TheoremÂ 4.33) we have
\begin{equation*} \exp(x)\exp(y) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \sum_{r+s= n} \frac{x^ry^s}{r!s!} \end{equation*}
By the binomial theorem
\begin{equation*} \sum_{r+s = n} \frac{x^ry^s}{r!s!} = \sum_{r=0}^n \frac{x^r y^{n-r}}{r!(n-r)!}= \recip{n!}\sum_{r=0}^n \binom{n}{r}x^r y^{n-r} = \recip{n!} (x+y)^n. \end{equation*}
