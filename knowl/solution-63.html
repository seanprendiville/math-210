Theorem 6.16. Intermediate value theorem.
Let \(f : [a,b] \to \R\) be a continuous function on a closed interval \([a,b] \subset \R\text{.}\) For any real number \(y\) between 3 \(f(a)\) and \(f(b)\text{,}\) there exists \(x \in [a,b]\) such that \(f(x) = y\text{.}\)
