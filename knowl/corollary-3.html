Corollary 4.25. Limit comparison test.
Let \((a_n)_{n=1}^\infty\) and \((b_n)_{n=1}^\infty\) be sequences of real numbers with \(b_n \gt 0\) positive for all \(n\text{.}\) Suppose that 2 
\begin{equation*} \lim_{n\to \infty}\frac{|a_n|}{b_n} \lt \infty \quad\text{and} \quad\sum_{n=1}^\infty b_n \lt \infty. \end{equation*}
Then \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n\) converges absolutely.
Proof.
