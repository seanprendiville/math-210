Definition 6.18. \(n\)th root function.
Let \(n \in \N\text{.}\) We define the function \(\sqrt[n]{} : [0, \infty) \to [0, \infty)\) by setting \(\sqrt[n]{a}\) to be the unique non-negative real number \(x \in [0, \infty)\) such that \(x^n= a\text{.}\) The existence and uniqueness of \(x\) are guaranteed by CorollaryÂ 6.17.
