Proposition 4.16. Integral test, proof deferred.
Let \(a,b \in \Z\) with \(a \lt b\text{.}\) Let \(f : [a,b] \to \R\) be a decreasing function 3 . Then
\begin{equation} f(a)+ \int_a^b f(t)\intd t \geq \sum_{n=a}^b f(n) \geq f(b)+ \int_a^b f(t)\intd t.\tag{4.3} \end{equation}
