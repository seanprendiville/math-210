Proposition 7.6. Sequential characterisation of differentiability.
Let \(f : (a,b) \to \R\) and \(x_0 \in (a,b)\text{.}\) Then \(f\) is differentiable at \(x_0\) with derivative \(y\) if and only if for any sequence \((a_n)_n\) in \((a,b)\setminus\set{x_0}\) with \(a_n\to x_0\) we have
\begin{equation} \frac{f(a_n)-f(x_0)}{a_n-x_0} \to y \text{ as } n \to \infty\text{.}\tag{7.1} \end{equation}
Proof.

We first prove the "\(\implies\)" direction. Let \((a_n)_n\) be a sequence in \((a,b)\setminus\set{x_0}\) such that \(a_n \to x_0\) as \(n \to \infty\text{.}\) We are required to prove (7.1), assuming [cross-reference to target(s) "eq-eps-diff" missing or not unique]. Fix \(\eps \gt0\text{.}\) Then by [cross-reference to target(s) "eq-eps-diff" missing or not unique] there exists \(\delta = \delta(\eps) \gt 0\) such that if \(x \in (a,b)\) and \(0\lt |x-x_0| \leq \delta\) then
\begin{equation*} \abs{\frac{f(x)-f(x_0)}{x-x_0} - y}\leq \eps\text{.} \end{equation*}
Since \(a_n \to x_0\) there exists \(N = N(\delta)\) such that for all \(n\geq N\) we have \(0\lt |a_n - x_0| \leq \delta\text{.}\) Hence if \(n \geq N(\delta)\) then
\begin{equation*} \abs{\frac{f(a_n)-f(x_0)}{a_n-x_0} - y}\leq \eps\text{.} \end{equation*}
This establishes (7.1).
Next we prove the "\(\impliedby\)" direction. To do so we prove the contrapositive. Hence, suppose that the negation of [cross-reference to target(s) "eq-eps-diff" missing or not unique] holds:
\begin{align*} (\exists \eps \gt 0)(\forall \delta \gt 0)\amp(\exists x= x(\delta) \in (a,b))\\ \bigsqbrac{\ 0\lt |x-x_0| \leq \delta\quad \text{and}\quad \abs{\frac{f(x)-f(x_0)}{x-x_0} - y} \gt \eps\ }\text{.} \end{align*}
Taking \(\delta = 1/n\) in the above negation, set \(a_1:= x(1)\text{,}\) \(a_2:=x(1/2)\text{,}\) \(a_3 := x(1/3)\text{,}\) etc. Then \(a_n \in (a,b)\setminus\set{x_0}\text{,}\) \(|a_n - x_0| \leq 1/n\) and
\begin{equation*} \abs{\frac{f(a_n)-f(x_0)}{a_n-x_0} - y} \geq \eps\text{.} \end{equation*}
Hence \((a_n)_n\) is a sequence in \((a,b)\setminus\set{x_0}\) with \(a_n \to x_0\) and
\begin{equation*} \frac{f(a_n)-f(x_0)}{a_n-x_0} \not\to y\text{,} \end{equation*}
(as required).
