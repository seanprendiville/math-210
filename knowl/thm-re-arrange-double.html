Theorem 4.26. Changing the order of summation.
For each pair \(i,j\in \N \cup \set{0}\) let \(a_{ij}\) denote a real number. Suppose that \(\displaystyle \sum_{i=0}^\infty\sum_{j=0}^\infty |a_{ij}|\lt \infty\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} \sum_{i=0}^\infty\sum_{j=0}^\infty a_{ij} = \sum_{j=0}^\infty\sum_{i=0}^\infty a_{ij} = \sum_{n = 0}^\infty \sum_{i+j=n} a_{ij}.\tag{4.7} \end{equation}
Proof (NON-EXAMINABLE).
